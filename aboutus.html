BIO VINYL

Bio Vinyl is an eco-friendly vinyl-selling business from Greensboro, NC. We currently have one store located in the Petty Building on the campus of UNC-Greensboro, but we hope to expand our company across the country someday. Our current focus is to lower the state of North Carolina's current pollution/ carbon emission into the environment by using recyclable materials for music purposes.
